福居良

Jazz Pianist from Japan

Scenery

Ryo Fukui (福居・良) was a self taught jazz pianist. He began teaching himself piano when he was 22 years old in 1970. Six years later, he released his debut, Scenery. He was able to achieve success in Japan. Although, the same cannot be said about other parts of the world. Nonetheless, Scenery has achieved masterpiece status. At a time when jazz was diverging in multiple, experimental directions, it was Ryo Fukui's more traditional sound that captivated audiences. While Ryo Fukui didn't reach the levels of fame as someone like Duke Ellington, his work, Scenery is still considered among the best of its decade.
